The songs of my playlist

Counting up our last winners Top 5 songs

By our last winner: Pamela Patrick

I was driving home from vacation the past month and the cities inevitable traffic, all ready tired from driving from city to city plus three hours in the border and voila! relief came to me in the form of a local radio station 105.7 fm Im sure many of you did listened to a lot of radio when young. Im sure you as I recorded songs on the cassete player trying to cut out the DJ Intros so that we had nothing but music on our mix tape. Anyways the radio had their top 5 Songs of all time, Well I have my own and a brief story of why became my top 5.

Blur - Girls and Boys.I like this song because it reminds me waking up to another city when I went in a backpacking trip whith my best friend to Mexico City. I remember what a relief from my exhausting trip.

The Smiths - There is a light that never goes out So another exhausting trip I had this time in San Francisco when a local bar played this song, but at the time there were no Song catcher so I lost the song, I didnt know who played it or the name of it, luciky I heard it again in a local bar from my town and with the smartphone, put in the song catcher and there it was. Never to lose a song again.

Echo and the bunnymen-lips like sugar. This song is a catchi song, It was played in a bar of Vancouver when I was playing bowls with my husband and it got me!, made feel in love again.

John waite-Missing young. Who hasn´t gone tru a heartbrake? well I was traveling to the Riviera Maya in my juvenile years when that happend. It makes me laught now! But sure this song will remind my bad mistakes and big No-no´s as a teenager.

Real Life -Send me an angel. I just to go to 80´s music night club and this song was the best!, Of course all are great but this is so nice to hear, I just love this song.
